Community Engagement

My First Community Engagement Project

On 3rd October, I had the privilege to embark on my first community engagement outreach at the New Legon AdMA Model JHS located in the Adenta Municipality.

Theme: "My Teen, My Life Today, My Future Tomorrow."

The session focused on:

  • Helping young learners understand teenage challenges
  • Building confidence and discipline
  • Making informed life decisions
  • Embracing a strong mindset toward their future

The event was peaceful, impactful, and well-received. I was honored to share the platform with Mr. Emmanuel Sasu, a laboratory technician, who also enlightened the students with an inspiring message.

This project marked a significant milestone in my journey of youth development and social responsibility — and it is only the beginning.
